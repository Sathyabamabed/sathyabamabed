About our College

The Sathyabama College of Education is now it is being Administrative by R. Rajendran, Chariman cum Managing Trustee of the institution.
The College also takes interest in developing the students to get placement opportunities by conducting special coaching classes for TRB, TNPSC and TNTET. Not only the present students but also the Alumni benefitted from these classes.
The Correspondent, Coordinator and Principal are of the feeling that growth of technology can lead to prosperity, wished that a strong foundation will help the growing buds to develop their all-round personality. This inspiration paved the way for the birth of this College of Education. Sathyabama College of Education gives all the possible coaching and improves Micro and Macro teaching skills. It conducts Citizenship Training Camp, Teaching practice, Sports Activities, Educational Tour and Field Trip.
The students of our institution are imparted not only Education but also training to develop various skills. For this noble cause seminars, Conference and workshops are conducted by inviting skilled persons.
The college has dedicated team of teachers and well stacked library, Educational Technology lab, communication lab, computer lab and psychology lab for the upliftement of the students. Transport facility is provided from all the areas. The prime mission of the Institution is to promote the features of Education, Discipline, Service mind, Patriotism and Social commitment and create teachers for the future society.
